]]> Welcome to VRWeb.IO

Technically speaking it’s been live for a little over a week now. But I’ve been endlessly tinkering around with every line of code within the site, trying to get it perfect before publicizing it.

VRWeb.IO is a virtual reality web design studio. It is the result of me wanting to jump into the world of virtual reality. My past mainly revolves around web design and when I started working with VR in the browser I knew it was going would become  something amazing and worth jumping into. So I decided I was done waiting for the future to begin on its own and do something about it myself.

My main goal is to bring VR Web Design to businesses, people, organizations, groups and whoever else interested in it both locally and beyond. It may take some time but I firmly believe that virtual reality is a new platform that will take off, much like mobile web a few years back, and people are going to want VR content!

I hope to begin adding more to  my portfolio page into the future as I come up with new ideas or maybe even make some websites. I’ve only got a couple at the moment but if you’re interested go check them out!

So where does it go from here?

I’ll try to write posts every once in a while about new things related with VR or VR on the web but nothing as in-depth as a news site. I also really want to start getting involved with the VR community, web related, games, experiences, videos and whatever else is out there. We are all in this together as early birds to the upcoming VR revolution. So feel free to message me or add me on social media!

Oh just a little side note if you missed it, the front page of vrweb.io is actually VR capable. If you view it on your phone you can look around or just click and drag the image. I bring it up because it’s my first attempt at integrating Virtual Reality Web with traditional and mobile web.
