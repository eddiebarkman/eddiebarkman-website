Hello, I'm Eddie Barkman.


"Who is this guy?" I hear you ask yourself.

Professionally, I am a multimedia artist and graphic designer. I have an Associates degree in Graphic Communication and a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Graphic Design(focus on Interactive Multimedia).

I've got experience in HTML, CSS/SCSS, Wordpress, and some knowledge in Javascript, JQuery, PHP and MYSQL. For video creation I use After Effects and Premiere Pro. I'm also quite familiar with the rest of Adobe's Creative Suite, including Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign.

Unprofessionally, I am a geek of all sorts and a hobbyist of anything creative and fun. I'm a big gamer that started young with a Super Nintendo and a Gameboy. These days I'm mostly a PC gamer!

Hobby-wise, I started playing violin back in fourth grade but moved onto piano later on. These days I dabble in both piano and guitar (how well it sounds though depends on who's listening.) I also really enjoy drawing characters (If you check out my portfolio, I have a ton!).

I've also been working on Virtual and Augmented Reality design. Check out a couple of my examples here and here.
