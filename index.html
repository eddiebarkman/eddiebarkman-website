History,

2006 | Intro to Web Design

I started majorly investing time in web design in High School as part my virtual enterprise class and began learning Macromedia Flash and early web development in College. This opened me to the field of Graphic Design.

2008 | Hobby Developer and Artist

I used what I learned to start building websites, making videos, and other creative digital projects. What started as just for fun became something I loved to do. This led to a YouTube channel with videos and hundreds of subscribers and many fun part-time projects!

2011 | Fresno City College

I began to learn Graphic Design principals and how to use professional software. Digital media creation became my passion, I loved building websites and creating content. At this time I earned an Assoicate in Science of Graphic Communications from Fresno City College. I knew this was what I wanted to do with my life, so I decided to pursue a four year degree.

2016 | California State University

I knew for certain Web and Graphic Design were where my passions were. I took my design work to the next level, learning new technologies and skills. I earned my Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design. My main focus being on design for interactivity and futuristic technologies like Virtual and Augmented Reality.

2017 | Graphic Design Freelance

After graduation, I began doing freelance design and web development while I search for the team I can become a part of and use my skills to their fullest! Meanwhile, I'm continuing to teach myself new things, staying up to date on new technologies. With so many new possibilities out there in VR, AR, and more, I can't wait to move forward into the future of design and development.

2018 - Current | JCS Marketing

I am currently the Senior Graphic Designer for JCS Marketing, Inc. I work on monthly print publications, including the leading tree nut publication West Coast Nut and its sister magazines. In addition to the magazines themselves, I've been responsible for designing print and web advertisements for clients, building and maintaining multiple websites, launching new publications, websites, and two successful agriculutre podcasts.

We also host multiple tradeshows and conferences throughout California. With the 2020 pandemic, I was tasked with building and running live online seminars and education.

Needless to say, my time at JCS Marketing has expanded not only my design skills has tested me and helped me develop skills in many different areas.
