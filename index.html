Hello, I'm Eddie Barkman.


"Who is this guy?" You ask yourself confusedly.

Professionally, I am a multimedia artist and graphic designer. I have an Associates degree in Graphic Communication and a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Graphic Design.

I've got experience in HTML, CSS/SCSS, Wordpress, and some knowledge in Javascript, JQuery, PHP and MYSQL. For video creation I use After Effects and Premiere Pro. I'm also quite familiar with the rest of Adobe's Creative Suite, including Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign.

Unprofessionally, I am a geek of all sorts and a hobbyist of anything creative and fun. I'm a big gamer that started young with a Super Nintendo and Gameboy. These days I'm mostly a PC gamer and proud 3DS player! I've also been a fan of anime and manga for well over a decade. And don't even get me started on movies and TV shows!

Hobby-wise, I started playing violin back in fourth grade but moved onto piano later on. These days I dabble in both piano and guitar (how well it sounds though depends on who's listening.) I also really enjoy drawing characters (If you check out my portfolio, I have a ton!).

I'm also beginning to work heavily on Virtual Reality. I'm working on how to design 360 experiences on the web. To find out more about that check out my website VRWeb.IO or contact me directly.
