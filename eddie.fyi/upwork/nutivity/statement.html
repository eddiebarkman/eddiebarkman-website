Writer's Statement

Growing up in India as a Hindu I was exposed to many strong female Deities, but knew very little about biblical characters. When I was first introduced to the Nativity Story, my initial thoughts were:

  1. Who asked Mary what she wanted?
  2. What if Mary had dreams of her own, wanted to be self-sufficient and career driven and that’s why God wanted her to have his Son—because she was unlike other women and could inspire them.

I decided to write the story from those premises, including people and religions from all walks of life to tell this age-old tale.

Prashant Thakker
